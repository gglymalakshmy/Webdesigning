Harry Potter and The Cursed Child

About The Book

Harry Potter and the Cursed Child is a two-part stage play based on the Harry Potter series written by J. K. Rowling, John Tiffany, and Jack Thorne.

About The Author

J. K. Rowling, is a British author best known for writing the Harry Potter series. She was born 31 July 1965 in Yate, Gloucestershire, England. Rowling's books have sold millions of copies worldwide and have been adapted into successful film franchises. In addition to her work as an author, Rowling is also known for her philanthropy and activism. She has received numerous awards for her contributions to literature and has inspired a generation of readers.
